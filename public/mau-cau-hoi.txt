[DUNG] Do thi ham so $y = \frac{2x+1}{x-1}$ co tiem can dung $x = 1$. | Vi diem ky di lam cho mau bang 0 va tu khac 0 la 1.

[SAI] The tich khoi lap phuong canh $2a$ la $4a^3$. | The tich cua khoi lap phuong canh $2a$ phai la $V = (2a)^3 = 8a^3$.

[DUNG] Cho so phuc $z = 3 - 4i$. Mo-dun cua $z$ la $|z| = 5$. | $|z| = \sqrt{3^2 + (-4)^2} = \sqrt{25} = 5$.

[SAI] Trong khong gian $Oxyz$, mat phang $(P): 2x - y + 3z - 1 = 0$ co vector phap tuyen la $\vec{n} = (2; 1; 3)$. | Vector phap tuyen dung cua mat phang la $\vec{n} = (2; -1; 3) \neq (2; 1; 3)$.

[DUNG] Tich phan $\int_0^1 e^x dx = e - 1$. | Ho nguyen ham la $e^x$, cho can $0 \to 1$ ta duoc $e^1 - e^0 = e - 1$.

Cho ham so $y = x^3 - 3x^2 + 2$ co do thi (C). Nhung menh de sau dung hay sai?
a. [DUNG] Ham so dong bien tren cac khoang $(-\infty; 0)$ va $(2; +\infty)$.
b. [SAI] Ham so dat cuc dai tai diem $x = 2$.
c. [DUNG] Do thi (C) cat truc tung tai diem co tung do bang $2$.
d. [SAI] Gia tri nho nhat cua ham so tren doan $[0; 3]$ la $-1$.
Giai thich: Dao ham $y' = 3x^2 - 6x$; $y'=0$ tai $x=0$ va $x=2$. Ham doi dau tu sang + qua 2 nen x=2 la diem cuc tieu (Y b sai). Kiem tra tung do: $y(0)=2$ (Y c dung). Tren doan [0;3], kiem tra ba vi tri x=0, 2, 3 thi $\min y = y(2) = -2 \neq -1$ (Y d sai).
---

---
Trong khong gian he toan do $Oxyz$, cho cac diem $A(1; 0; 0), B(0; 2; 0), C(0; 0; 3)$.
a. [DUNG] Phuong trinh mat phang $(ABC)$ la $\frac{x}{1} + \frac{y}{2} + \frac{z}{3} = 1$.
b. [SAI] $\vec{AB} \cdot \vec{AC} = 0$.
c. [DUNG] The tich khoi tu dien $OABC$ bang $1$.
d. [SAI] Khoang cach tu $O$ den mat phang $(ABC)$ bang $1$.
Giai thich: Y a la ptmp theo doan chan. Y c dung cong thuc $V = \frac{1}{6} \cdot 1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 = 1$. Y d sai do khoang cach nghich dao la $\frac{1}{h^2} = 1 + \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{9} = \frac{49}{36} \Rightarrow h = \frac{6}{7}$.
---

---
Cho so phuc $z$ thoa man phuong trinh $(1+i)z = 3 - i$.
a. [SAI] Phan thuc cua $z$ bang $2$.
b. [DUNG] Phan ao cua $z$ bang $-2$.
c. [DUNG] Diem bieu dien so phuc $z$ nam tren goc phan tu thu $(IV)$ cua mat phang toa do.
d. [SAI] So phuc lien hop cua $z$ la $\bar{z} = -1 - 2i$.
Giai thich: Bien doi phuong trinh ta duoc $z = \frac{3-i}{1+i} = 1 - 2i$. => Phan thuc bang $1 \neq 2$ (Y a sai). Phan ao la $-2$ (Y b dung). Toa do diem la $(1; -2)$ thuoc goc phan tu $(IV)$ (Y c dung). So lien hop la $1 + 2i$ (Y d sai).

Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve ester va lipid:
a. [DUNG] Ethyl acetate thuoc nhom ester.
b. [SAI] Phan ung xa phong hoa la phan ung thuan nghich.
c. [DUNG] Triglyceride la triester cua glycerol voi fatty acid.
d. [SAI] Lipid chi gom triglyceride.
Giai thich: Xa phong hoa la mot chieu. Lipid gom triglyceride, wax, phospholipid, steroid.
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve carbohydrate:
a. [DUNG] Glucose la aldohexose.
b. [SAI] Fructose la aldose.
c. [SAI] Sucrose co tinh khu.
d. [DUNG] Maltose co tinh khu.
Giai thich: Fructose la ketose. Sucrose khong co nhom hemiacetal tu do nen khong co tinh khu.
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve amine va amino acid:
a. [DUNG] Aniline la aromatic amine.
b. [DUNG] Glycine la hop chat luong tinh.
c. [DUNG] Lien ket peptide co dang -CO-NH-.
d. [SAI] Protein khong duoc tao tu amino acid.
Giai thich: Protein la polymer cua amino acid duoc lien ket boi lien ket peptide (-CO-NH-).
---

---

d. [DUNG] Nylon-6,6 thuoc nhom polyamide.
Giai thich: Teflon la polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), khong phai polyamide.

c. [SAI] Copper khong day duoc silver khoi dung dich AgNO3.

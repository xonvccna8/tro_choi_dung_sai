# CACH GO CONG THUC HOA HOC:
# - Chi so duoi: go binh thuong
#   Vi du: H2SO4 hien thi thanh H₂SO₄
#   Vi du: C6H12O6 hien thi thanh C₆H₁₂O₆
#
# - Chi so tren (ion, dien tich): dung ^{...}
#   Vi du: Fe^{2+} hien thi thanh Fe²⁺
#   Vi du: SO4^{2-} hien thi thanh SO₄²⁻
#
# - Mui ten phan ung: -> (→), <-> (⇌)
# - Delta: (delta) → Δ
# - Do: (deg)C → °C
#
# LUU Y:
# - Dong bat dau bang # la ghi chu (se bo qua)
# - De trong dong giua cac cau hoi cho de doc
# - Dap an: [DUNG] hoac [SAI] (viet hoa)
#
# ========================================

## CAU DON DUNG/SAI
# Format: [DUNG] hoac [SAI] Menh de | Giai thich

[DUNG] Glucose co cong thuc C6H12O6. | Glucose la monosaccharide thuoc nhom aldohexose.

[SAI] Fructose la aldose. | Fructose la ketose (co nhom C=O o C2), khong phai aldose.

[DUNG] Ethyl acetate (CH3COOC2H5) thuoc nhom ester. | Ethyl acetate la mot ester pho bien co mui thom.

[SAI] Phan ung thuy phan ester trong moi truong base la thuan nghich. | Xa phong hoa trong moi truong NaOH/KOH la phan ung mot chieu.

[DUNG] Amine la dan xuat cua NH3 khi thay H bang goc hydrocarbon. | Day la dinh nghia co ban cua amine.

[SAI] Aniline (C6H5NH2) la amine beo. | Aniline la aromatic amine vi co vong benzen.

[DUNG] Protein la polymer cua amino acid. | Cac amino acid lien ket voi nhau bang lien ket peptide tao protein.

[SAI] Cellulose tan tot trong nuoc. | Cellulose khong tan trong nuoc va nhieu dung moi huu co.

[DUNG] Sucrose la disaccharide khong co tinh khu. | Sucrose khong con nhom OH hemiacetal tu do.

[SAI] Polyethylene duoc tao tu phan ung trung nguong. | Polyethylene duoc tao tu phan ung trung hop CH2=CH2.


## CAU 4 Y DUNG/SAI
# Format: Giua 2 dau ---
# Dong 1: Cau hoi
# Dong 2-5: a/b/c/d. [DUNG] hoac [SAI] Noi dung
# Dong cuoi: Giai thich: ...

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve ester va lipid:
a. [DUNG] Ethyl acetate thuoc nhom ester.
b. [SAI] Phan ung xa phong hoa la phan ung thuan nghich.
c. [DUNG] Triglyceride la triester cua glycerol voi fatty acid.
d. [SAI] Lipid chi gom triglyceride.
Giai thich: Xa phong hoa la mot chieu. Lipid gom triglyceride, wax, phospholipid, steroid.
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve carbohydrate:
a. [DUNG] Glucose la aldohexose.
b. [SAI] Fructose la aldose.
c. [SAI] Sucrose co tinh khu.
d. [DUNG] Maltose co tinh khu.
Giai thich: Fructose la ketose. Sucrose khong co nhom hemiacetal tu do nen khong co tinh khu.
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve amine va amino acid:
a. [DUNG] Aniline la aromatic amine.
b. [DUNG] Glycine la hop chat luong tinh.
c. [DUNG] Lien ket peptide co dang -CO-NH-.
d. [SAI] Protein khong duoc tao tu amino acid.
Giai thich: Protein la polymer cua amino acid duoc lien ket boi lien ket peptide (-CO-NH-).
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve polymer:
a. [DUNG] Polyethylene duoc tao tu monomer ethylene (CH2=CH2).
b. [DUNG] PVC la poly(vinyl chloride).
c. [SAI] Teflon la polyamide.
d. [DUNG] Nylon-6,6 thuoc nhom polyamide.
Giai thich: Teflon la polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), khong phai polyamide.
---

---
Cho cac nhan dinh sau ve dien hoa:
a. [DUNG] Trong pin galvanic, electron di tu anode sang cathode.
b. [DUNG] An mon dien hoa cua iron lien quan pin vi mo.
c. [SAI] Copper khong day duoc silver khoi dung dich AgNO3.
d. [DUNG] Aluminum duoc dieu che bang dien phan nong chay Al2O3.
Giai thich: Cu day duoc Ag vi Cu hoat dong manh hon Ag trong day dien hoa.
---
